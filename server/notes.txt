How to start mongoDB ->

In terminal: (MAC OS)
1: mongod
2: mongo --host localhost:27017

(Windows)
1: "C:\Program Files\MongoDB\Server\5.0\bin\mongod.exe" --dbpath="c:\data\db"  (http://127.0.0.1:27017/)
2: https://www.mongodb.com/docs/manual/tutorial/install-mongodb-on-windows/ or https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xwr3azx0Gf0

1: mongosh.exe ( mongodb://127.0.0.1:27017/)
